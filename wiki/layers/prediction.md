# Prediction

Forward simulation. Before Brain acts — or before [[learning]] updates a belief — it generates an explicit expected outcome. The delta between prediction and reality is the signal that powers [[learning]] and [[curiosity]].

## Drive
Accurate forward simulation. Prediction quality IS Brain's intelligence metric — the curve of "predictive accuracy week over week" is the proof of compounding.

## Architecture: BRAID graphs

**[[braid]] (arXiv:2512.15959, OpenServ)** — Mermaid-graph bounded reasoning.

Key insight: a frontier model generates the reasoning graph **once**, then a cheap local model **executes** it. The architect draws the blueprint; the worker executes; the blueprint is cached forever.

This collapses Brain's two inference paths into one workflow:
- **Slow path** (Claude API): generate BRAID graphs for novel reasoning patterns
- **Fast path** (local 8B Llama/Mistral on the gaming desktop): execute cached graphs

Result: frontier-model reasoning quality at near-zero marginal cost.
